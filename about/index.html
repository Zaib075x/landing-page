About Us.

Here at Implifi, we emphasize practical learning and strongly agree that every CS student must possess valiant set of skill to shine in the IT industry. We believe that the right usage of skills will lead to improved workplace performance.

We provide the lab environment to promote practical learning where any students can access the content remotely and also analysis tools and progress for teachers to evaluate the performance of students.
